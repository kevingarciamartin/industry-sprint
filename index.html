  • Time management

    Time management is about organizing your time efficiently to prioritize important tasks. By setting clear goals and creating a schedule, you can break big tasks into smaller, manageable ones. Focus on one task at a time, avoid multitasking, and take regular breaks to stay productive. Delegating tasks when possible and regularly reviewing your progress helps you stay on track and meet deadlines effectively.
